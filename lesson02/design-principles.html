Erin Stewart

White Space and Clean Design

Costa Vida

costavida.com

Costa Vida's homepage uses white space and clean design well. The white space helps the page feel uncluttered. Each element stands out in a way that is easy to understand. The pops of color, the order buttons, and the image look appealing against the white background.

PARC: Contrast

Hulu

hulu.com

Hulu uses contrast to make their homepage pop. The dark background makes their signature green color stand out. The green buttons really pop, encouraging users to click and sign up. The white text is also easy to read against the background.

PARC: Repetition

Audible

audible.com

Audible's homepage divides sections by using repetition. The repetition makes it easy to evaluate each section quickly. The bestsellers and new releases are displayed in a row, with repeating image and text elements. The next section highlights different categories to explore. The category icons are displayed in a grid, with repeating text, icon, shape, and background elements.
